# 🫀 Cardiovascular Case Study: Heart Failure ### 🔍 Clinical Manifestations > **Left-Sided (L = Lungs)** | **Right-Sided (R = Rest of Body)** > :--- | :--- > 🌬️ Dyspnea & Orthopnea | 🦵 Dependent Edema (Legs/Sacrum) > 🔊 Crackles/Wheezes | 🎈 Jugular Venous Distension (JVD) > 🩸 Frothy Pink Sputum (Edema) | 🤢 Abdominal Distension/Ascites ### ⚠️ Risk Factors & Diagnostics * **Risks:** Hypertension, Myocardial Infarction, CAD. * **Labs:** **BNP > 100 pg/mL** (The "gold standard" for HF stretch). * **Imaging:** Echocardiogram (Check **Ejection Fraction**; Normal is 55-70%). ### 💊 Interventions & Meds 1. **Furosemide (Lasix):** Diuretic. *NCLEX Tip: Monitor Potassium levels!* 2. **Digoxin:** Increases contractility. *Hold if HR < 60.* 3. **Positioning:** High-Fowler's to ease breathing. 4. **Fluid Restriction:** Usually < 2L/day.
